# Mystic Grove Playtest Instructions

Build: Playtest 01

## How To Launch The Build
1. Open `builds/windows_playtest/`.
2. Double-click `MysticGrove_Playtest_01.exe`.
3. Use `Play` for a first run or `Continue` if you already have a save.

## What To Test First
Start with the core loop:
1. Open Flower Grove.
2. Wait for Stored Mana to increase.
3. Collect Mana.
4. Upgrade Flower Grove if you can afford it.
5. Return to the village.
6. Open Fairy House and assign a fairy.
7. Open Sacred Pond and restore it.
8. Open Potion Shop, craft a potion, then sell it.
9. Open Quests and claim any completed reward.
10. Save, close, reopen, and continue.

## Playtest Length
Each playtest should take about 5-10 minutes.

## How To Report Bugs
Use `data/BUG_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md`.

Include:
- What happened.
- What you expected.
- Steps to reproduce.
- Screenshot or video link if possible.
- Severity.

## Features Not Included Yet
- Potion Shop has only one recipe.
- No inventory screen.
- No daily quests or events.
- No fish breeding.
- No monetization.
- No multiplayer.
- No combat.
- Art and sound are placeholder.
