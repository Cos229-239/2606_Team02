# Mystic Grove Demo Notes

Build: Demo Build 01

## What The Game Is
Mystic Grove is a cozy idle village game about restoring a magical fairy grove.

## Core Loop
Flower Grove produces Mana. The player collects Mana, upgrades the Flower Grove, assigns fairies for bonuses, restores the Sacred Koi Pond, crafts potions for Coins, and claims quest rewards.

## Current Features
- 2.5D-style Main Village.
- Flower Grove mana generation.
- Flower Grove upgrades and plot unlocks.
- Fairy House worker assignment.
- Sacred Pond restoration and level rewards.
- Grove Restoration visual progression.
- Potion Shop crafting and selling.
- Quest system with claimable rewards.
- Save/load.
- Main menu with Play, Continue, Reset Save, and Quit.

## What Is Playable
- Collect Mana.
- Upgrade Flower Grove.
- Unlock a Flower Grove plot.
- Assign fairies to Flower Grove or Sacred Pond.
- Restore the Sacred Pond.
- Craft and sell one Mana Potion recipe.
- Complete and claim starter quests.
- Save and continue progress.

## What Is Placeholder
- Art is lightweight placeholder art.
- Potion Shop has one recipe.
- Fairy roles are simple bonuses, not advanced AI.
- Pond rewards are prototype bonuses and placeholders.
- Audio is not final.

## Known Limitations
- No inventory screen yet.
- No daily quests or events.
- No fish breeding.
- No monetization.
- No multiplayer.
- No combat.
- The UI is designed for prototype readability, not final art direction.

## Suggested Demo Flow
1. Start from the Main Menu.
2. Enter the Main Village.
3. Open Flower Grove and collect Mana.
4. Upgrade Flower Grove.
5. Open Fairy House and assign a fairy.
6. Open Sacred Pond and restore it.
7. Open Potion Shop, craft a potion, and sell it.
8. Open Quests and claim a reward.
